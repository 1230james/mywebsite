Welcome!

Welcome to 1230james's Website! My name is James Hyun, a college undergraduate majoring in computer science who likes to program and do some other niche things.

This website is intended to serve as an online "portfolio" where I can show off the things I want to share to anyone who passes by, from online acquaintances to potential employers to family members.

View the links in the navigation bar above to see and learn more about who I am and what I do.

This website is still a work-in-progress!
Some pages may be incomplete or have their contents changed without notice.
